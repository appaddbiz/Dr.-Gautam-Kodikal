About Me

Dr. Gautam Kodikal, MBBS, MS (Ortho), is a highly experienced Senior Consultant Orthopedic Surgeon with a distinguished career spanning decades in orthopedic surgery, joint replacement, and pediatric orthopedics. A graduate of Armed Forces Medical College and a postgraduate from Christian Medical College Ludhiana, he has trained internationally in advanced orthopedic techniques, including arthroscopy and the A.O (Swiss) Indo-German Fellowship. Dr. Kodikal has served in prestigious institutions like Mallya Hospital, Fortis Hospitals, and Nova Specialty Surgery, and has been actively involved in teaching, research, and clinical leadership. Renowned for his compassionate care and surgical precision, he remains a trusted expert in delivering comprehensive orthopedic solutions.
