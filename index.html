Understanding Avascular Necrosis
A Serious Bone Condition That
Needs Timely Intervention

Avascular necrosis (AVN) is the death of bone tissue due to a lack of blood supply. It often affects the hip and may lead to joint destruction if untreated. Early detection and personalized treatment are essential for better outcomes.

Consult with AVN Expert

Dr. Gautam Kodikal — A senior orthopaedic surgeon with deep expertise in managing Avascular Necrosis through both conservative and surgical interventions.
